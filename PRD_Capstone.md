# Capstone Project: Op-Ed Writing and Publication

## Overview
The platform culminates in a high-stakes capstone project where students write an op-ed piece that gets reviewed by a professional editor and published in a local newspaper. This creates ultimate extrinsic motivation through real-world validation and publication.

## Project Structure

### Preparation Phase
- **Skill Building:** Students progress through ranks, mastering all writing traits
- **Topic Selection:** Choose from current events, local issues, or personal passions
- **Research Matches:** Special matchmaking for gathering supporting evidence
- **Draft Iterations:** Multiple competitive drafts with peer feedback

### Writing Phase
- **Extended Timeline:** 45-minute writing session (longest in platform)
- **High-Stakes Scoring:** Maximum points available for exceptional work
- **Expert Feedback:** AI provides advanced editorial-style feedback
- **Revision Cycles:** Multiple rounds of improvement based on feedback

### Editorial Review Process
- **Professional Editor Matching:** Platform partners with local newspapers
- **Blind Review:** Editors assess writing without knowing student identity
- **Detailed Feedback:** Comprehensive editorial notes and suggestions
- **Publication Decision:** Editors select pieces worthy of publication

### Publication Achievement
- **Local Newspaper Publication:** Real byline in print and online editions
- **Platform Recognition:** Special "Published Author" achievement and ranking
- **Community Impact:** Writing influences local discourse and decision-making
- **Portfolio Highlight:** Capstone piece becomes permanent portfolio centerpiece

## Extrinsic Motivation Elements

### Reward Structure
- **Publication Bonus:** 500 points for newspaper publication
- **Editorial Praise Points:** Additional points based on editor feedback quality
- **Community Recognition:** Local publicity and social media sharing
- **Career Advancement:** Writing samples for college applications or jobs

### Competitive Aspects
- **Publication Tournament:** Seasonal competition for limited publication slots
- **Editorial Rankings:** Track which students receive highest editorial praise
- **Publication Streaks:** Multiple publications earn special status
- **Influence Metrics:** Track real-world impact of published pieces

## Learning Science Alignment

### Mastery Demonstration
- **Holistic Assessment:** Requires integration of all five writing traits
- **Real-World Application:** Authentic writing for genuine audience
- **Transfer Skills:** Applies platform-learned skills to novel context
- **Self-Regulated Learning:** Students manage extended writing process independently

### Motivation Research
- **Ultimate Extrinsic Reward:** Publication provides tangible, prestigious outcome
- **Social Proof:** Real publication validates writing competence
- **Achievement Unlocking:** Capstone unlocks "elite" platform features
- **Legacy Building:** Creates permanent record of writing achievement

## Technical Implementation

### Editorial Network
- **Newspaper Partnerships:** Contracts with local publications nationwide
- **Editor Database:** Qualified journalists with writing expertise
- **Review Platform:** Secure system for editorial feedback submission
- **Publication Pipeline:** Automated coordination with newspaper production

### Quality Assurance
- **Editorial Standards:** Platform maintains high publication quality
- **Diverse Topics:** Ensures variety of voices and perspectives published
- **Ethical Guidelines:** Prevents harmful or inappropriate content publication
- **Impact Tracking:** Measures community response to published pieces

## Success Metrics
- **Publication Rate:** Percentage of capstone attempts resulting in publication
- **Quality Scores:** Average editorial ratings of published pieces
- **Student Satisfaction:** Surveys measuring pride in published work
- **Community Impact:** Citations and discussions generated by publications

This capstone creates the ultimate extrinsic motivation: real-world publication with professional validation, transforming writing from academic exercise to meaningful public contribution.
