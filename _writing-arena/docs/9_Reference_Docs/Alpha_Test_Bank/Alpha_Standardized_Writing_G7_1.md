## Reading Passage: The Story of the Solar-Powered Village

**Paragraph 1**  
In a small rural town, far from the bustling cities, electricity was once scarce. For years, families relied on candles and kerosene lamps for light. The arrival of solar panels changed everything. A community-led project, funded by a mix of donations and government support, brought rows of panels to the rooftops of every home.

**Paragraph 2**  
Children could now study in the evenings without straining their eyes. Shops stayed open later, boosting local business. Even the small medical clinic, which once closed at sunset, could store vaccines and operate equipment at night.

**Paragraph 3**  
However, the transformation was not without challenges. Some residents worried about the costs of maintaining the panels. Others feared the technology might fail during cloudy weeks. To address these concerns, the project leaders organized workshops on panel care and energy-saving habits. Over time, these sessions built both knowledge and trust.

**Paragraph 4**  
Today, the village stands as a model for other communities seeking affordable, renewable energy solutions. Visitors come to learn from their experience, and residents take pride in their role as pioneers. ## Essay Prompt

Write an **expository essay** explaining how the solar panel project improved life in the village.

Use information from the passage to describe at least three different ways the project changed the community. Organize your ideas clearly and support them with details from the text.

### Make sure your response includes the following:

**Write 5 clear paragraphs:** Introduction with a strong thesis, 3 body paragraphs with topic sentences, and a conclusion (5 points)

**Use evidence and explain it:** Support each point with facts or examples from the passage and explain what they show (5 points)

**Stay organized and on topic:** Use transitions, keep your ideas in order, and focus on the main idea (4 points)

**Use strong sentences:** Vary sentence types and use clear, formal language (3 points)

**Edit your writing:** Check your spelling, punctuation, and grammar (3 points)
