
You'll be given a topic. Your task is to write topic sentences for that topic.

Your topic sentence should work as the first sentence of a paragraph on the topic. Try to engage the reader by presenting the topic in varied and interesting ways.

You may be given a recommended strategy, like using an appositive, or writing an interrogative sentence. It's up to you to decide whether to use the recommended strategy or not.

## Examples:

In this example, we are given the topic "Solar System":

{{example_question}}

> Write a topic sentence for the following topic: Solar System.

Below are some topic sentences you might write to introduce a paragraph related to the Solar System:

{{example_solution}}

> Write a topic sentence related to the topic.
> Without the Sun, Earth would not be able to support life.

> Write a topic sentence. Recommendation: use an appositive.
> The smallest planet in the Solar System, Mercury, is also the closest to the Sun.

> Write a topic sentence. Recommendation: write an interrogative sentence.
> What makes the Solar System different from other star systems?
